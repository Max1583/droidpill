5 Best android video editing apps in 2021 10mins

There are more than 4 million applications on the Google Play Store. In fact, hundreds of apps are coming out everyday which makes it really hard for any user to keep a track.

Finding the best Android apps from the ocean of apps available on the Play Store can be a challenge and that is where we come in! We test various apps across different categories and bring you my Top 10 Best Android Apps August 2020.

5. Power Director

Power Director is a highly advanced video editing app that allows users to splice apps, arrange clips and add titles. This video editing app for Android also comes with a timeline feature, video effects, transitions and voice over capabilities.

A user will have to pay a fee to get rid of watermarks and they will also be able to export material at 1080p. This is a good movie editing app for video.

    Features
  • Edit and export videos in up to 4K resolution*

  • Trim, splice and rotate videos with simple taps

  • Apply jaw-dropping effects and transitions with drag & drop

  • Combine pictures and video in one clip using the multi-timeline

  • Add text or animated titles to your video in seconds

4. VideoShow

VideoShow has received numerous awards and is undoubtedly one of the best video editing apps for Android available on the Google Play Store for free.

Apart from the essential functions, you can also beautify your video by adding text, effects, music and sound effects or perform live dubbing.

    Features
  • The soundtrack of a video can be converted to mp3.

  • Multiple background music can be added to a video.

  • Blurred background, options to adjust audio speed and voice enhancement features

  • 50 elaborate themes to beautify videos, vlogs, and slideshows
Download

3. FilmoraGo

FilmoraGo is a remarkable Android video editor app that is liked by many users.
You can make square 1:1 videos for Instagram, 16:9 videos for Youtube, create reverse videos, add transitions, slow motion, text, etc. to beautify your video.

    Features
  • Clips can be previewed in real-time.

  • Photos and videos can be imported from social media websites like Facebook, Instagram, etc.

  • An extensive collection of templates and effects.

  • A set of professional editing tools.
Download

2. Adobe Premiere Rush

Adobe Premiere Rush enables you to edit any video right from your Android device quickly. It is fast and fun to use.
Apart from the standard video editing functions, you can apply video effects, change color, create pan and zoom effects, add custom made animated titles and more.

    Features
  • Easily the all-in-one video editor app for Android users.

  • One can add a thumbnail, schedule post for TikTok, YouTube etc.
Download

1. KineMaster

Combined with a well-designed interface along with powerful features, KineMaster is one of the good video editing apps available on Android.
KineMaster offers remarkable levels of control over the editing process to create professional videos quickly.

However, the removal of the watermark and full access to premium assets can only be accessed if you purchase the professional edition of the app.

    Features
  • Multiple layers of videos, images, text, effects, overlays, stickers, and handwriting can be combined.

  • Instant preview feature for viewing the editing

  • Precise volume control from moment to moment within a clip
Download
